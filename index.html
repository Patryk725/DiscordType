Why should you use discord?
Play and chat with your friends
Discord is made to play with friends, you can create a server and chat with them. You can also talk using microphone by voice chat. You can learn how to add your friends on discord here.
Control and develop team projects
Plan projects now, granting permissions to server members, create thematic channels to avoid making a mess. Click to learn more.
Make friends and develop your passion
A huge community uses Discord. You can meet new people, exchange information and join a thematic server to develop your passions. Learn here, how to join a server.
